About Me

My educational background includes associate degrees in Math/Science, Humanities, Liberal Arts Studies, and Social/Behavioral Science from Mt. San Jacinto College in San Jacinto, California; a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from National University in La Jolla, CA; and a Certificate in Web Development from UC Davis in Davis, California; and a master's degree in Information Technology with a concentration in Web Design at Southern New Hampshire University in Manchester, New Hampshire.

I enjoy membership in such national organizations as the American Association for the Advancement of Science, the National Society of Leadership and Success, the Interaction Design Foundation, and the Society for Technical Communication. I have also held memberships with with both the Association for Computing Machinery and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers.

I am currently employed as a junior application developer in the IT department at Broadridge Advisor Solutions in San Diego, CA.
